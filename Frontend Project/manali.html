Manali Tour Package

Package Overview

Experience the beauty of Kullu-Manali, one of India's most scenic hill stations. This package includes accommodation, sightseeing, and adventure activities.

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Manali

Pickup from Delhi/Chandigarh and transfer to Manali. Check-in to your hotel and rest. Evening free for leisure.

Day 2: Solang Valley Adventure

Visit Solang Valley for adventure activities like skiing, paragliding, and snowboarding.

Day 3: Local Sightseeing

Explore Hidimba Temple, Manu Temple, Mall Road, and Tibetan Monastery.

Day 4: Departure

Check out from hotel. Return to Delhi/Chandigarh.

  • Daily breakfast & dinner
  • Local sightseeing
  • Pickup & drop from Delhi/Chandigarh
